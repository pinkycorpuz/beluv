BeLuv Child Safety Standards

Last updated: July 8, 2026

BeLuv is an adults-only dating app. Users must be at least 18 years old to create or maintain an account, and minors are not permitted to use the service.

CSAE and CSAM Prohibition

BeLuv prohibits child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, sextortion, trafficking, sexualization of minors, or any content or behavior that exploits, abuses, endangers, or targets a child.

Users may not upload, share, solicit, promote, store, or distribute CSAM or any content involving minors in a sexual or exploitative context.

Age Restrictions

BeLuv requires date-of-birth information and blocks users who do not meet the 18+ requirement. Accounts found to belong to minors may be blocked, restricted, removed, or reported where required by law.

In-App Reporting

Users can report safety concerns from within BeLuv using the report tools on profiles and discovery surfaces. Users may also block other users to prevent further interaction.

Reports are reviewed for safety enforcement, including account restriction, account bans, content removal, and escalation where legally required.

CSAM Response

When BeLuv obtains actual knowledge of CSAM or CSAE-related conduct, we take appropriate action in accordance with these standards and applicable law. This may include removing content, preserving safety records, disabling accounts, and reporting confirmed CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) or the relevant regional authority where required.

Child Safety Contact

Child safety concerns, including CSAE or CSAM reports, may be sent to beluv.support@gmail.com.

This contact is intended for child safety and abuse-prevention escalations, including potential notices from Google Play.
