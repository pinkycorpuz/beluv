BeLuv Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information BeLuv collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you when you use the app.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your username, recovery email, birthdate, profile details, profile photos, chat messages, reports, and account settings.

We may also process technical and usage information needed to operate the app, such as authentication records, device notification tokens, and activity related to matches, messages, and moderation actions.

How We Use Information

We use your information to create and maintain your account, show your profile to other users, deliver matches, send messages, support notifications, and personalize discovery features.

We also use information to protect users, investigate abuse, review reports, enforce safety rules, and comply with legal obligations.

Sharing and Visibility

Your profile information is shared with other users as part of the dating experience. This may include your name, age, bio, photos, interests, location details you choose to provide, and relationship preferences.

We may share information with service providers that help us run the app, such as authentication, database, storage, analytics, and notification providers, only as needed to operate BeLuv.

Safety and Moderation

BeLuv includes safety tools such as blocking, reporting, moderation review, and account enforcement for users who violate community or safety rules.

Reports, internal moderation notes, and related enforcement records may be retained for safety, fraud prevention, and legal compliance purposes.

BeLuv prohibits child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM). Child safety concerns may be reported in the app or by email at beluv.support@gmail.com.

Your Choices

You can update your profile information in the app, manage notification preferences on your device, block or report other users, and delete your account from Settings.

When you delete your account, BeLuv will attempt to remove your profile, matches, messages, and sign-in account according to the backend deletion workflow.

Children and Age Restrictions

BeLuv is intended only for adults. Users must be at least 18 years old to create or maintain an account.

Accounts found to belong to underage users may be removed or restricted.

Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain security, resolve disputes, enforce policies, and satisfy legal requirements.

Some records, especially moderation and abuse-prevention records, may be retained longer where needed to protect the community.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes may be reflected in the app, in release notes, or through other appropriate notices.
