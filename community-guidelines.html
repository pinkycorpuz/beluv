BeLuv Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 8, 2026

BeLuv is an adults-only dating app. These guidelines define the conduct and content standards expected from every user.

Respect and Consent

Treat other users with respect. Harassment, threats, bullying, stalking, hate speech, impersonation, coercion, and unwanted sexual pressure are not allowed.

Adult-Only Community

Users must be at least 18 years old. Minors may not create accounts, appear in dating profiles, or participate in chats or discovery.

Photo and Content Rules

Profile photos, messages, and uploaded content must not include nudity intended to be sexually explicit, non-consensual intimate content, violence, illegal activity, scams, spam, or content that exploits or endangers another person.

Child Safety

BeLuv strictly prohibits child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, sextortion, trafficking, sexualization of minors, or any conduct that exploits, abuses, endangers, or targets a child.

Reports and Enforcement

Users can report profiles, photos, messages, and safety concerns in the app. BeLuv may remove content, restrict features, ban accounts, preserve moderation records, or report confirmed CSAM to NCMEC or the relevant regional authority where required.

Safety issues may also be sent to beluv.support@gmail.com.
