BeLuv Account Deletion

Last updated: June 19, 2026

This page explains how BeLuv users can request account and data deletion.

Delete Your Account in the App

If you can sign in to BeLuv, open the app and go to Settings, then choose Delete Account. This starts deletion of your profile, matches, messages, and sign-in account through the app deletion workflow.

Request Deletion Without App Access

If you cannot access the app, send a deletion request to support from the email address connected to your BeLuv account.

Contact: beluv.support@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Your BeLuv username, if known.
  • The email address used for your account.
  • A short note saying you want your BeLuv account deleted.

What Gets Deleted

Account deletion is intended to remove:

  • Your BeLuv profile information.
  • Your profile photos and uploaded account media.
  • Your matches and chat messages.
  • Your sign-in account where deletion is supported by the backend.

What May Be Retained

Some records may be retained when reasonably necessary for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, payment records, dispute handling, or abuse and moderation history.

Processing Time

In-app deletion begins immediately when the request is confirmed. Email deletion requests are reviewed manually and are normally processed within a reasonable time after account ownership is verified.
